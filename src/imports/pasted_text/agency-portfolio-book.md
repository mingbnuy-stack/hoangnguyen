Create a highly curated, editorial-style agency portfolio book. The canvas size must be A4 Portrait (W: 794px, H: 1123px per frame). Generate 4 distinct pages (frames) that flow sequentially.

ART DIRECTION & MOOD:
- Style: "Refined Brutalism" meets "Architectural Blueprint." 
- Color Palette: Strictly monochromatic. Backgrounds are stark white (#FFFFFF) or off-white paper (#F4F4F0). Text and structural elements are deep black (#111111) and neutral gray (#888888).
- Typography System: Create extreme tension. Use a hyper-refined, elegant Serif for primary headlines (e.g., Instrument Serif, Playfair Display). Pair this with a rudimentary, utilitarian Monospace or strict Grotesk for body copy and metadata (e.g., Space Mono, Roboto Mono, or Inter).
- The "Elevator" Indexing: Every page must feature a massive, oversized, raw utilitarian number (e.g., "01", "02") anchored in the top left corner or bleeding off the left edge, acting as a fixed floor/section indicator.
- Grid & Structure: Use a visible, rigid 4-column or 12-column layout. Embrace immense negative space. Separate sections with raw 1px solid black lines. Do not use drop shadows, gradients, or soft edges. 

PAGE EXECUTIONS:

Page 1: The Foundation (Formerly Hero Section)
- Elevator Index: "00" (Massive, top left).
- Layout: Immense negative space at the top. Content anchored to the bottom half.
- Headline: "End-to-End Solutions for Brand Growth." (Refined Serif, massive font size, tightly tracked).
- Subheadline: "We are a full-service agency that combines strategic foundation, asset building, amplification, and sales performance to deliver comprehensive marketing and design solutions." (Monospace, small, strict block alignment).
- Call-to-Action: Represent the "Buttons" as rudimentary, high-contrast editorial badges. Two sharp-edged black rectangles with white text: "[ VIEW OUR WORK ]" and "[ BOOK A CONSULTATION ]".

Page 2: The Flow (Systematic Approach)
- Elevator Index: "01" 
- Layout: A horizontal, architectural timeline spanning the middle of the page, connected by a continuous 1px black baseline. 
- Content blocks distributed equally along the line:
  - Step 1: Foundation (Strategy & Visual Identity)
  - Step 2: Asset Building (Digital Platforms & Content)
  - Step 3: Amplification (Events & Public Relations)
  - Step 4: The Destination (Sales & Conversions)
- Styling: Use very small, uppercase monospace typography for the steps. Treat them like structural annotations on a blueprint.

Page 3: Core Services (Pillars 1 to 4)
- Elevator Index: "02"
- Layout: A strict 3-column architectural grid. Instead of a web accordion, use a brutalist list format. Draw 1px horizontal black dividers between each service. 
- For each item below, format the Title in bold, the "Goal & Direction" in standard text, and "The Benefit" slightly indented or in an italicized serif to show the refined/rudimentary contrast.
- Item 1: Strategic Consulting & Planning. Goal: Market research, uncovering core insights, and establishing an overall strategic map. Defining the vision for long-term objectives. Benefit: Provides businesses with a clear development roadmap, optimizing resources and ensuring the right direction.
- Item 2: Branding & Visual Identity. Goal: Creating the core visual identity, from visual design to brand language. Benefit: Builds a unique, professional image that helps the brand stand out and leaves a powerful impression.
- Item 3: Digital Ecosystem Development. Goal: Building, designing, and standardizing online touchpoints. Establishing a solid technological infrastructure. Benefit: Clients own a modern digital estate that delivers an optimal User Experience.
- Item 4: Digital Content Creation & Management. Goal: Producing multimedia content formats aligning perfectly with the core message. Managing distribution. Benefit: Maintains continuous interaction, attracts potential customers, and conveys the brand story.

Page 4: Core Services (Pillars 5 to 7)
- Elevator Index: "03"
- Layout: Continue the exact same 3-column rigid grid and horizontal 1px dividers from Page 3 to maintain editorial consistency. Leave the bottom right quadrant of the page entirely blank (negative space) for visual relief.
- Item 5: Event & Experiential Marketing. Goal: Conceptualizing and coordinating events. Translating messages into physical, spatial experiences. Benefit: Creates direct engagement, amplifies emotions, generates industry buzz, and drives word-of-mouth.
- Item 6: Public Relations & Reputation Management. Goal: Executing press releases, directing media narratives, and establishing public opinion monitoring. Rapid-response crisis protocols. Benefit: Absolutely protects reputational assets, commands public opinion, and solidifies trust.
- Item 7: Sales Activation & Performance Solutions. Goal: Designing and executing marketing campaigns geared toward tangible results. Benefit: Shortens the buyer's journey, creates breakthroughs in revenue, and delivers the highest ROI.

Ensure all padding and margins are mathematically consistent. Make it feel like a high-end printed object translated to a screen.
